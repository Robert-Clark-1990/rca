Frequently Asked Questions

Have you ever wanted to ask a stranger a question? Well, chances are someone has already asked it, so why not peruse the following questions to see if they scratch that proverbial itch?

At the moment, my books are pretty much exclusive to Amazon. I’d love to have them in a bookstore, but doing so is mighty difficult in my situation, so for now you’ll have to hurl some money in Amazon’s direction.

You can find all the books on my Amazon author page: Amazon UK Amazon US

My goal is to make each book accessible to a newcomer, but then I went and wrote The Fate of Glass as a direct sequel, which kind of threw things out the window. My advice would be either to read them in order of release, or chronologically.

The ark I have in mind for these books will occasionally result in some books being part of a more focused series, but I endeavour to write others that are standalone James Stone stories.

I’ll try to remember to update this when I release new books, but for now (the great British year of 2020) the chronological order of James Stone books is:

  • The Killing Business
  • The Glorious Dead
  • The Damned and The Buried
  • Aftermath
  • Impact
  • Ricochet
  • No More Shadows
  • The Line
  • The Fate of Glass
  • Second Solace

At time of writing, No More Shadows is the only book I have made available on Audible. This isn’t because I don’t want to. I listen to audiobooks almost every day, and get through way more of them than I do physical books, but getting them done is time consuming and costly, so until I’m making a proper profit from writing, they unfortunately have to be on the back burner for now.

I don’t sell signed copies because I think it’s a bit unfair to charge you lovely readers more money for something that amounts to a squiggle on a page. However, if you own a physical copy of my books and happen across me at an event, then I’d be more than happy to sign/selfie/spill my innermost secrets to you! (maybe not that last one).

How incredibly kind of you to ask. You can pop a review on your friendly neighbourhood Amazon storefront, or Goodreads, or anywhere else that allows you to review my books!

And thanks for doing that, you lovely son of a gun you!

Unfortunately, you’ll have to overthrow a government.

Where aren’t they set? (actually, quite a lot of places, so I’ll answer the question properly)

So far, I’ve taken James to Afghanistan, England, America, Canada, Bruges and France, and he shows no sign of slowing down!

I try to reign it in as much as possible, but I do sometimes talk about chickens (get it, fowl language???) I’m a hoot. (a little more for you there)

Seriously though, I do have some swear words in. If that bothers you, you probably won’t like all the torture, murder, theft, violence and sarcasm that I manage to fit in around the F’ing and Jeffing.
